This study reveals how experiences are encoded by brainwide reprogramming of synaptome architecture. Using synaptome mapping tools, the research demonstrates that experiences reshape the molecular and structural composition of synapses across the entire brain, providing new insights into how the brain stores and processes experiences at the synaptic level.
