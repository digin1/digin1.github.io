A key step in such studies involves registering molecular images to reference brain atlases, a process hindered by the difficulty of accurately delineating brain regions. Toward this, we have curated a collection of high-resolution, dual-fluorescence microscopy images, termed as dual-fluorescence mouse brain microscopy (DMBM) dataset, complemented by expert annotations of 118 subregions.
